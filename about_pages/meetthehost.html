Meet the Host

John Wilson III

John R. Wilson III is a dedicated voice of Truth in a world filled with confusion and deception. While once on a path far from godliness, he found himself—by the grace of God—unable to buy into the lies of postmodernism that were being taught at the university he attended. At the age of twenty, during an intense internal struggle with the questions of life that led to external poor decisions, the Lord began to deal with his heart. On August 5th, 2007, John repented of his sins, was baptized in Jesus’ name, and received the baptism of the Holy Ghost, speaking in other tongues as the Spirit gave utterance. This transformative encounter led him to leave his former lifestyle and wholeheartedly pursue God and His Truth.

His hunger for answers—both spiritual and practical—ignited a passion for understanding the world, politics, and culture through a biblical lens. This pursuit of Truth resulted in him leaving the university and embracing the God-given call to ministry. Over the years, he has been deeply involved in pulpit ministry, street outreach and evangelism, home Bible studies, and Sunday school teaching. He spent two and a half years evangelizing full-time across many regions, sharing the gospel, and has pastored multiple churches. Additionally, he has written articles for magazines, contributed to online radio programs and podcasts, and engaged in both biblical and secular debates.

Now, as the founder of Revealed Radio Network, John R. Wilson III continues his mission of unveiling the Truth of Scripture and exposing the realities of the world. His work is dedicated to pulling the masks off deception—both spiritual and natural—and revealing Truth to all who have ears to hear.
