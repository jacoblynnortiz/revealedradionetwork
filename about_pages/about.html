About RRN

The Truth Shall Set Us Free

In the turbulent year of 2020—amidst the chaos of a contentious presidential election, racial division fueled by deception, the COVID-19 madness, the rise of humanism seeking to replace Christianity, the relentless “trust the science” propaganda, the grip of an emerging draconian government, and the outright demonization of godliness—God began to deal with Pastor John R. Wilson III in a deeply personal way.

At a trying time in his life, while hospitalized and spending quiet moments with the Lord, as his loving family, faithful friends, and devoted church members lifted him in prayer, God placed a calling on him. It was a call to step out, to take the risk, to stand boldly in the face of criticism and hatred—all for the sake of Truth.

Revealed Radio Network was not born out of ambition but out of necessity. It arose from an unshakable conviction, an inability to remain silent in the face of widespread deception, and an undeniable mandate to be a trumpet of Truth in a world drowning in lies. This network exists to unveil biblical realities, expose the darkness, and equip believers with knowledge—both spiritual and natural—so they can stand firm in faith, discern the times, and never bow to the lies of this age.

John Wilson III

Founder & CEO

Our Mission

In an age where deception runs rampant and darkness seeks to veil the minds of men, the Revealed Radio Network stands as a beacon of unshaken truth. We are more than a broadcast—we are a movement. Through an arsenal of voices, research, literature, online learning, biblical studies, roundtable discussions, and news, we equip seekers with the wisdom of God’s Word and the discernment to navigate the realities of our time.

Here, the masks of deception are torn away, exposing the schemes of the adversary and illuminating the path of righteousness. We do not merely report—we reveal. We do not just discuss—we declare. This is not a place for passive learning but for active awakening.

From the depths of Scripture to the breaking headlines of the day, from kingdom strategies to prophetic insights, Revealed Radio Network exists to inform, empower, and embolden the people of God to stand unwavering in Truth—both spiritual and natural. The time for blindness is over. The hour for revelation is now.
